HERITAGE
Born from racing DNA.
Unveiled at the 2013 Geneva Motor Show, LaFerrari represents Ferrari's first foray into hybrid hypercar technology. The name itself — simply 'LaFerrari' — was chosen to signify the definitive Ferrari, a car that embodies everything the marque stands for.
With only 499 coupés and 210 Apertas ever produced, ownership transcends acquisition. It is entry into one of motorsport's most exclusive circles.
We chose to call this model LaFerrari because it is the maximum expression of what defines our company.
— Luca di Montezemolo
